A host of household debt and housing concerns have devalued the Canadian dollar by 3.0% since last year, relative to the US dollar. Compared to the other 19 countries in this list, Canada is the picture of an average-priced summer vacation. Per-mile flights cost more in Canada, especially in the more northern regions, but the proximity to major domestic airports makes it the cheapest place to fly in total. The Great White North is in the middle quartile in nearly all other cost categories.
