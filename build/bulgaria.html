Bulgaria

Average Vacation Expenses in Bulgaria

-3.81%

— value growth in dollar since 2017

The exchange rate between the dollar and the Bulgarian Lev has worsened by 3.81 percent from 2017 to 2018, making an otherwise quite affordable European vacation destination less attractive this year. As with many other nations, relatively stable internal politics and economic policy, combined with a fickle U.S. leadership, has led to appreciation in local currency values against the dollar. While airfare is cheaper than elsewhere in Europe, and commodity prices remain low, this year might be one to take a pass on Bulgaria due to the weakness of the dollar. Another year, the same price might fetch a more luxurious getaway.
